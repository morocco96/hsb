About Us

About Us

For people living with Diabetes

Hajiya Safinatu Buhari Foundation “for people living with Diabetes” is a Non Governmental, Non Partisan and Non Profit Making Organization dedicated in memory of our Dear Mother, Sister, Grandmother and Former First Lady (Federal Republic of Nigeria of Nigeria). Hajiya Safinatu Buhari.

HSBF is a Foundation deeply committed to alleviating the suffering of people living with Diabetes in Nigeria with special emphasis on the poor who cannot afford the Hight Cost of Managing Diabetes.

Vision/Mission Statement

Vision

To live in a world free of Diabetes

Mission

Promoting Diabetes Care and Prevention

Motto

Uniting for Diabetic people

  • Prevention
  • Awareness
